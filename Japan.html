Japan; 日本

Places I find fascinating in Japan

Tokyo Tower

Did you know that the Tokyo tower is actually taller than the Eiffel tower? Did you also know that the tower gets repainted every 5 years? And apparently, they change its color on special occassions such as Christmas. Personally, I am a such fan of the Tokyo tower.

Meiji Shrine

This shrine hosts festivals throughout the year and is a very popular place. Many weddings also take place at this shrine. I witnessed one myself. Did you know that the Meiji shrine burned down during World War II? It was later rebuilt thanks to public donations.

Shibuya

It is known as the busiest intersection in the world. This is a place where hundreds of people cross at a time. Especially on a Friday night, this place is packed with people. It's awesome!

Asakusa

Is it just me or are these temples awfully stunning? This is actually a very famous Buddhist temple. It is also the oldest temple of Tokyo.
